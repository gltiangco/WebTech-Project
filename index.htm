About Me
Welcome to Spend it in Sweden! My name is Gabbi, a student who spent the Spring of 2019 on an exchange program in Lund, Sweden. During this time, I had the great pleasure of calling 3 different cities my home having lived in Lomma, studied in Lund and spent weekends hanging out in Malmo. This website catalogs my entire semester, covering the must-knows of living in Sweden to the tips and tricks of traveling in Europe, to an explorer's guide to some yummy food and topnotch activities. Enjoy!
